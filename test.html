so future

Dogecoin is the latest and fastest growing digital currency system. Dogevertise aims to turn them now into a way for internet users to not have to see ads. While on a Dogevertise-enabled website, a small amount of the users' processing is used to mine DOGE, giving their payouts to the website they were on. This pays sites for page views based on time spent on the website, and gives internet browsers a completely ad-less experience on the web.
